Architecture Deep Dive

Every detail,
by design.

Scroll through the architecture that makes X1 Pro and X1 Max unlike anything else.

2nm Process

The smallest.
The most powerful.

Built on TSMC N2 — the world's most advanced process node. 12 billion transistors packed into the X1 Pro die. 24 billion in X1 Max. All at 2nm precision.

CPU Architecture

12 cores.
Zero compromise.

10 Performance cores and 2 Efficiency cores in X1 Pro. Each P-core hits 5.2 GHz sustained. The E-cores handle background tasks at a fraction of the power.

GPU Architecture

8K video.
In real time.

20-core GPU on X1 Pro. 64-core on X1 Max. Hardware-accelerated ProRes, H.266, and AV2 encode and decode. Edit 8K60 without breaking a sweat.

Neural Engine

AI runs here.
On-chip. Private.

16-core Neural Engine delivering 19 TOPS in X1 Pro, 32-core at 38 TOPS in X1 Max. Run 7B parameter language models locally. No cloud. No latency.
